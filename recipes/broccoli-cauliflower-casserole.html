Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole

Description

Broccoli and cauliflower casseroles are the spice of life at every event! Be careful, you might cause your guests to beg for more servings after they have a taste of this!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese (divided)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 (15 oz) package seasoned croutons (crushed)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place broccoli + cauliflower in separate saucepans.
  3. Beat eggs in bowl until creamy.
  4. Bake in preheated oven.
  5. Enjoy!
