Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the perfect Italian meal for anyone who would like to get their home-cooking skills up! Follow this recipe to make the most gorgeous meal you will have ever seen in your life!

Ingredients

  • cheese filling
  • meat
  • sauce
  • spinach

Steps

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Prepare meat sauce.
  3. Combine cheese mixture.
  4. Layer & bake.
  5. Enjoy!
